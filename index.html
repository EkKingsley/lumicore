Process

How we work with your data & teams

Simple, transparent, and measurable—from the first data extract to live dashboards and in-store execution.

01
Discover & Align

We clarify your business questions—promo ROI, pricing, space optimization, distribution—and audit data sources (POS, ERP, promo calendars, brand plans).

02
Engineer & Analyse

Promo efficiency

+18% promo uplift for a leading brand

Country-wide promo review across 3 modern trade chains, 2 years of POS data.

  • Modelled baseline vs incremental sales across 70+ frozen SKUs.
  • Identified “hero” SKUs and promo mechanics with negative ROI.
  • Re-designed promo calendar and support by retailer & region.

Outcome: +18% average uplift on key frozen SKUs and −20% spend on low-return promotions in the following quarter.

Category & Brand Management

11% category growth from data-driven shelf & gondola layouts

Multi-store optimisation for brands and categories in retail chains and distrubutors.

  • Calculated SPPD, ND/WD, and VMS% to rank SKUs by contribution.
  • Recommended facings, delists, and line extensions by format.
  • Delivered planogram guidelines and a field execution checklist.

Outcome: +11% value growth in focus categories and improved on-shelf availability on top 40 SKUs.

Pricing Stragety

Clear rules for when to discount—and when not to

National biscuits and snacks brand with heavy promotional spend but unclear returns.

  • Mapped all promo types (TPR, bundles, multi-buy, displays).
  • Estimated price elasticity by store cluster and brand tier.
  • Designed a "promo decision playbook" for the marketing & sales team.

Outcome: fewer but smarter promotions, with a 2.6× average ROI on the new promo mix.

About LumiCore

A data partner for Brands, Retailers & Distributors

We speak both languages—analytics and brand. That’s how we bridge the gap between Excel sheets and in-store execution for Manufacturers, Distributors, and Retailers.

LumiCore was founded in Lebanon by analysts and marketers who were tired of seeing strong Brands, Retailers and Distributors lose at the shelf because decisions weren’t grounded in data.

We’ve worked with manufacturers, distributors, and retailers across categories—from frozen poultry and fries to biscuits, dairy, and beverages—helping them understand what really drives performance in each channel and store.

  • Fluent in SQL, Python, Excel, DAX, and BI tools.
  • Hands-on experience with POS, ERP, and promo data.
  • Comfortable in the boardroom and in the field with merchandisers.
